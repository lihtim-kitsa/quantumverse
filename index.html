Quantumverse Vol. 3.0

IBM Qiskit Fallfest 2025

November 7th-9th // BITS Hyderabad

Register Now & Dive In

Enter the Era of Quantum Utility.

QuantumVerse Vol. 3 is a unique three-day event hosted by the Quantum Feynatics Group and IBM Quantum. This immersive experience, combining a hands-on workshop with a competitive hackathon, is your gateway to the latest advancements where practical quantum applications are finally becoming a reality.

A Century of Quantum!

From abstract theory to tangible technology, from pure curiosity to powerful computation. Join us at Qiskit Fall Fest to celebrate 100 years of quantum mechanics and be part of the generation that builds its future.

The Minds Behind The Verse

Quantum Feynatics Group is dedicated to exploring the fascinating world of quantum tech. We aim to make quantum knowledge accessible while building a vibrant community of innovators.

IBM Quantum is a global leader in advancing quantum computing, making the technology accessible worldwide through hardware, software, and tools like Qiskit.
